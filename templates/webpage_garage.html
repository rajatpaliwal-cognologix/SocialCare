From Social Distance to Social Care

June 16,2021
Image

SocialCare

The idea behind SocialCare is to utilize social Media to check on friends and colleagues if they feel negative.Twitter being a medium of intellectual discussion and small sentences, picking up sentiment of even socially less active twitter members get easy. SocialCare utilizes tweets and applies sentimental Analysis on the tweet.Taking the pattern of last few tweets, it can give a good idea if feelings if many tweets are negative in a row.


Who must utilize its potential?

June 16, 2021

Companies to Support their Employees

Understanding how employees are facing any issue have got difficult during this large scale Work from Home Experiment. It has a good side with people able to attend and enjoy with their families. However,many people are also facing lonelyness and depression.If we are able to determine these issues before they become persistant, it can be very helpful. The Dashboard of SocialCare can be used to glance on tweets by employees, their sentiments, words used as well as weightage of words in the tweet. For this we are using visualizations using plotly with the help of Expert.ai API.
